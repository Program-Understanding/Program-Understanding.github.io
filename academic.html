Samuel is an Associate Research Professor at Auburn. Prior to entering academia, he spent 17 years doing research in cyber security, vulnerability assessment, and binary program analysis at Sandia National Labs. Research Interests: binary program analysis, adversarial analysis, reverse engineering, machine learning, AI, virtual reality, combinatorial optimization problems
